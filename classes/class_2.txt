Drink class

Attributes:
size (integer) # in ounces 
straw (boolean)
alcohol_content (float)
ingredients (array of strings)
mixed_drink (boolean)

Methods:
make_double (increase alcohol_content by 100%)
shake_drink (toggles mixed_drink to true)
increase_size (increases size attribute by 5 ounces)
add_ingredient (add element to the end of ingredients array)
