Entrees class

Attributes
ingredients (array of strings)
hot_entree (boolean)
calories (integer)
to_go_box (boolean)
price (float)

split_entree (divide calories attribute by two)
remove_ingredient (remove element from ingredient array)
add_protein (adds extra protein: adds 3 to price attribute and ingredient to ingredients array)
box_entree (puts entree in a box: toggle to_go_box)
